\begin{exercise}

Consider the sequences $\{a_n \}_{n=1}$ and $\{b_n \}_{n=1}$, where $a_n =\frac{(\ln(n))^8+5^n}{n+4^n}$ and $b_n = \left(\frac{5}{4}\right)^n$.  Then:
\[
\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{a_n}{b_n} = \answer{1}
\]

\end{exercise}
